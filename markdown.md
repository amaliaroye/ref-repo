## Styling Text
| Style | Example | Syntax | Shortcut
| --- | --- | --- | ---
| Bold | **bold text** | `** **` | <kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>B</kbd>
| Italic | *italic text* | `* *` | <kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>I</kbd>
| Strikethrough | ~~strikethrough text~~ | `~~ ~~` | <kbd>Alt</kbd> + <kbd>S</kbd>
| Subscript | H~2~O
| Superscript | super^duper^
